# E-Commerce-sales-dashboard-Power-bi

**Objective:**
Build an interactive e-commerce dashboard in Power BI to visualize sales, profit, andquantity metrics.

**KPIS:**
**Total sales amount :**  ₹438K 
**Total Profit :** ₹37K
**Units sold :** 6k
**Total orders:** 500 
**Average order value :** ₹875.5

**Profit by Month:** Highest profits were recorded in November (₹10.3K) and February (₹9.7K), while a few months such as May and June showed losses.

**Sales by State:** Maharashtra (₹102K) and Madhya Pradesh (₹87K) are the top-performing states.

**Top Customers:** Major contributors include Harivansh (₹9.9K) and Madhav (₹9.4K) in sales.

**Profit by Sub-Category:** Highest profits came from Printers (₹8.6K) and Bookcases (₹6.5K).

**Quantity by Category:** Clothing (4K units) dominates sales volume, followed by Furniture and Electronics (1K each).

**Payment Methods:** COD (2K orders) and UPI (1K orders) are the most used payment modes.

# Key Insights

1.The business generated ₹438K in total sales and ₹37K in profit from 500 total orders, with an average order value of ₹875.5.

2.6K units were sold overall, with Clothing contributing the highest quantity (4K units).

3.Maharashtra (₹102K) and Madhya Pradesh (₹87K) recorded the highest sales among all states.

4.Printers (₹8.6K) and Bookcases (₹6.5K) were the most profitable sub-categories.

5.Top customers — Harivansh (₹9.9K) and Madhav (₹9.4K) — contributed significantly to total sales.

6.November and February were the most profitable months, while May and June saw losses.

7.COD (2K orders) and UPI (1K orders) were the most preferred payment methods.
